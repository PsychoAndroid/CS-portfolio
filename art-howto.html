1:Ellipse

This is just one of the many shapes that can be used in Javascript. Now to make a circle is simple, just type in ellipse((type in xPosition),( type in Yposition),(type in Length),(type in Width)) as an addition you can add stroke,strokeweight, and fill if you want. Now you might ask yourself what is stroke,strokeweight, and fill? Well its really simple, stroke is the line that surrounds the circle and the colors can be changed. There are three positions in stroke(color)(moveTo)(lineTo) Strokeweight on the other hand can increase or decrease the thickness or width of the stroke. To type in a strokeweight all you have to do is type instrokeWeight(). Finnally is fill, the purpose of fill is to just fill in the circle give it any color just try it out. Type in Fill(*any #,any #,any #) to simply choose a color.
